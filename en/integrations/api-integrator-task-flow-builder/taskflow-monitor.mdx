---
title: "TaskFlow Monitor"
description: "Enables viewing log records of Task Flow flows and accessing task and action log details. When a flow starts running, log records of the flow itself, tasks in the flow, and actions begin to be kept. Reported logs of all flows and their details can be accessed by applying filters through the dashboard page."
---
